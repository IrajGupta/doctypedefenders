How to Protect Yourself from these Aliens

  1. If you see an alien, please notify 202-370-3164 and send your location


  2. Once a warning has been issued in or near your area about the sightings of aliens, immediately move from your house to the closest bunker. Ensure children and elderly are sheltered in them.


  3. If you see anyone under attack, please hit the alien on its nape. However, only do so when you are in the blind spot of the alien. After doing so, please run to the nearest bunker as hitting them on the nape will only temporarily shock them.


  4. If you see more than one or a group of aliens together, do not panic. Immediately find a place to hide and make no sound, they are very sensitive to sound.
